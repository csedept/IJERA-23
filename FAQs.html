frequently asked questions

  • 1. What is the scope of the journal?

    We cover a wide range of topics within the computing domain, including but not limited to High Performance Computing and Simulation, Advanced Computing, Software Engineering, Intelligent Systems and Soft Computing Techniques, Image, Signal and Speech Processing, Internet modelling and Communication Systems, Intelligent Control, Robotics and Automation. Check our Aims and Scope section for more details.

  • 3. What types of publications do you accept?

    We accept a variety of publication types, including original research articles, reviews, case studies.

  • 4. How is the review process conducted?

    Our rigorous peer review process ensures unbiased evaluation of submitted manuscripts. Reviewers assess the scientific quality, originality, methodology, and relevance of the work. Authors receive constructive feedback to enhance their research.

  • 5. How long does the review process typically take?

    The review process duration can vary depending on several factors, including the complexity of the manuscript and the availability of reviewers. On average, authors can expect to receive feedback within [X to Y] weeks from submission.

  • 6. How do I contact the journal for inquiries or support?

    For inquiries, support, or collaboration proposals, please reach out to our team through our Contact Us page or email us at ijera@amaljyothi.ac.in
